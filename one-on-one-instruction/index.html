One-on-One Instruction

Live Fire Instruction Now Teaches Privately!

Could your shooting game use a little tweaking, or are you a brand new shooter? We are here to help! With our sixty-minute lesson you will gain a practical and working understanding of the fundamentals. Live Fire Instruction works out of the NRA Range, located in Fairfax, VA.

Register Below

What You Will Learn

The first lesson will cover the following:

  • Aiming
  • Trigger Control
  • Follow Through
  • Breath Control
  • Hold Control

Each additional lesson will continue to build on the fundamentals of pistol shooting and introduce defensive pistol techniques.

  • Pricing: One Student (1 hr) – $75.00/ Two Students (1hr total)- $125.00
  • Group rates are available (up to 4 people)- Please inquire.
  • Pricing does not include ammo and applicable NRA Range fees. Supplies and general merchandise can be purchased at the NRA Range.
